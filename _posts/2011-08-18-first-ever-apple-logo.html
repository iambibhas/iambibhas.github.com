This is their first logo - [caption id="" align="alignnone" width="468" caption="Apple Inc. - First Logo"][/caption] Click on it for a Higher resolution[1958 x 2457] version. This logo was designed by Ron Wayne, the designer of Apple Inc at that time. The logo features Sir Isaac Newton sitting under an apple tree. It was inspired by a quotation by Wordsworth that was also inscribed into the logo that said:
“Newton… A Mind Forever Voyaging Through Strange Seas of Thought... Alone.”
